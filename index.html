What Do I Do?

Oh hey there, thanks for stopping by my site. My name's Kelly and I'm a social media all things digital guru. I graduated from THE Ohio State Unviersity in 2013 and began my career in Chicago, where I worked in experiental marketing for Paragon Marketing Group. After gaining some experience, I packed my bags and moved to New York City where I honed in on my digital chops at RF|Binder and most recently Edelman Digital.

I've worked on a wide array of clients including Johson & Johnson, CVS, Wines of Germany, Talenti Gelato, and Samsung. From Tumblr to Twitter to Google Analytics and everything in between, I've mastered the art of digital marketing.

When I'm not working, you can find me cheering on my Buckeyes, running, and most definitely eating.
